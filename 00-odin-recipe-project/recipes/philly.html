Philly Cheesesteak

Description

The sandwich out of Philadephia, Pennsylvania is one loved by many outside of the state! This sandwich is warm and jam packed with flavors compliment each other perfectly.

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast beef

  • Onions

  • Banana peppers

  • Dijon mustard

  • Olive oil

  • Hoagie bread

  • Provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Cut meat into thin strip pieces

  2. Chop up your onions and banana peppers

  3. Place olive oil onto a stove top pan until sizzling

  4. Place your cut pieces of meat and vegetables onto the pan

  5. Cook and stir the food at high temperature until the meet is cooked well done

  6. Turn off your stove and let the hot food cool off

  7. Toast your hoagie and spread dijon on it>

  8. Place warm food on the bread and top that with cheese until melted and dig in!
