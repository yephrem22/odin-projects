Tibs

Description

Tibs is a popular Ethiopian dish that is full of flavor. It is eaten with injera and consists of beef cubes cooked with other spices for flavor

Ingredients

  • Chuck roast beef

  • Onions

  • Jalepeno

  • Rosemary

  • Olive oil

  • Injera

Steps

  1. Cut meat into small cubes

  2. Chop up your onions and jalepenos

  3. Place olive oil onto a stove top pan until sizzling

  4. Place your cut pieces of meat and vegetables onto the pan also adding your rosemary

  5. Cook and stir the food at high temperature until the meet is cooked well done

  6. Turn off your stove and let the food sit to cool off before enjoying this flavorfull dish!
