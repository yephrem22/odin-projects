Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a italian dish that consists of layers of spaghetti noodles that are flat, sauce, and cheese. Many people put their own twists on to the disk in terms of the top layer and what the sauce consists of.

Ingredients

  • Flat noodles

  • Sauce

  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place layers of flat noodles, sauce, then cheese on top of each other in a pan x3

  2. Heat oven to 360 and let cook for 45 minutes

  3. Take out and let it cool for a 10 minutes

  4. Cut and serve your Lasagna!
